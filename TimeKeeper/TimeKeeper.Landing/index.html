About

TimeKeeper

The complete solution for large-scale businesses looking to connect with their teams and deliver projects on time. TimeKeeper contains tools for employees to enter their work progress and employers to manage teams, business information and reports.



Gigi School of Coding

TimeKeeper project is completely done by trainees of Gigi School of Coding bootcamp. It is organized and hosted by Mistral Technologies. Through the school, trainees have the opportunity to acquire the latest knowledge of the entire software development process during the 3 months of training.
